About The Event

TuniHack
What is tunihack ? ? It’s a software engineering hackathon, to be held in National School of Computer Science(ENSI) on december 02th and 03th 2023, that will give developers the opportunity to put into practice their coding-skills to solve real-world engineering challenges. But wait !! there is a SURPRISE in this year's edition which is PitchHack

PitchHack : In this context, PitchHack is a new kind of marathon where teams choose a problem related to our theme, develop an adequate solution then make a pitch to present their work in front of our juries. The challenge is open for students and enthusiasts from any field

Gamification X Innovation


Gamification is like adding game elements to make everyday tasks more fun and engaging. When you mix this with innovation (coming up with new and better ideas), cool things happen. Imagine turning learning or work tasks into a game with points, rewards, and challenges. This makes people more motivated and creative. It's like playing a game where you need to solve problems and come up with smart ideas. This mix also encourages a culture of always learning and improving. People get excited to try new things and take on challenges. For example, events like hackathons bring together people to compete and solve problems quickly, just like in a game. In short, gamification and innovation together make problem-solving and learning more exciting and effective. It's like turning regular stuff into a game, and that makes everything more fun and productive

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this cost money?

Yes, 45 dinars by team (for the hackathon competition) and 20 dinars by team (for the PitchHack competition) .

who Can attend?

The event is open for everybody. Everyone is welcomed to attend the keynote speeches, the workshops, the discussion panel and the animation all night long.

Who have to register ?

the registration is for the hackathon (the programming competition). Otherwise, the participation in the different conferences and workshops requires no registration

How can I register ?

Just fill in the form provided below ! Since we have a limited groupe number, your motivation will be very important in the selection .

How can I register in PitchHack?

A register form will be open on 20th november 2023 , so all you have to do is fix your idea and prepare a presentation in which you explain your solution to the problem that you choose

What if this FAQ didn’t answer my questions?

Please email us at ossec.contact@gmail.com if you have any concerns at all! Don't worry, we don't bite. 😄

Event Rules

Registration: online registration ends November 27 at Midnight, or when maximum event capacity is reached.

Selection: Each group receives a response regarding his candidacy then candidates are invited to upload their CV in the form . If selected, a confirmation email is sent to the team, along with all the necessary information regarding the event.

Team requirement: teams may be composed of minimum 3 and maximum 4 eligible participants.

No Offsite development: all developments should be performed on-site between the start date and the end date of the event.

OpenSource: participants are permitted to use only open source tools/code.

in order to follow the work of teams, receive assistance by our technical consultants and prevent any sort of cheating, teams are required to spend the 24 hours in the hackerspace.
We do not have rights to any of your code. You own your code. Every team will have a maximum of 8 minutes to present their solution. Only one shot is allowed. Selecting winners: don't worry, your solution will be judged by professional software engineers. Detailed ranking criteria will be exhibited during event day.
